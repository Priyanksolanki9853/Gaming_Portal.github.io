Hello, I'm Priyank Solanki!

Welcome to my personal gaming portal. I am an independent game developer passionate about creating unique and engaging experiences. My journey began with a simple idea: to build worlds where players could lose themselves in creative challenges.

🕹️ My Philosophy: Creative Challenge

My games are built around the core principle of **creative challenge**. Whether it's the fast-paced physics of **Sharp-Shooter**, the strategic planning needed in **Mythical Dungeon**, or the demanding precision of **Escape**, I focus on mechanics that are easy to learn but difficult to master. I love blending diverse genres to create something fresh and unexpected.

I develop all the code, design all the levels, and craft the majority of the assets right here. This portal is a direct window into my imagination and dedication to the craft of game development.

🛠️ Key Skills & Technologies

Here are some of the tools and technologies I use to bring my game worlds to life:

  • Unity Engine (C#)
  • HTML5 / JavaScript (for web-based games)
  • Blender (3D Modeling)
  • Aseprite (Pixel Art)
  • Game Design & Level Design
  • Problem Solving & Optimization
